Who I am:

Sincere Girl. Dedicated Learner. Adventourous Dreamer.

Inspired by all of the people and experiences I encounter everyday


Here goes my story:

On the evening of October 5th, 2005, I was born into this world. My parents named me in words they wish to represent my soul and my believes. Since a child, I was trying to get answers, explore the little things around my neighborhood, and I would look at the constellations of the star at the countryside crystal sky. I'd prefer reading time with my mom and homework time inside the house, rather than running around, unlike most kids. While slowly growing up, I learn about the people around me, the lives they live in the country side and the challenges they face. I used to wonder, if life can be better, if there are answers to there ever-lasting problems, and if there is the solution that can bring perfectionism to their lives. If there is one thing I learn from these 14 years struggling joyfully through the puzzling maze of life, I learn that perfectionism is not that solution, but continuous effort can. The efforts we make, those small little things we do, can ultimately bring powerful contributions to the world we live in. Everyone is making a difference, whether to their own lives, their own destinies, their identities, their own families, and ultimately the world.

This world is full of positivities, courage and strength, and I want to be a part of it.









My Life in Liger Leadership Academy

In Liger, I am travelling from Who I used to be to Who I will become as a

Positive Change Agent

Liger is not just a school, but a family that we grow together as friends and learners. Through living together, we have a chance to create close connections and bonds that allow us to live happily as a community. We are kind, empathetic, and helpful to each other. Living together from many different places and cultures requires a whole level of understanding and adaptation, which I really appreciate from my fellow friends. We learn frome each other, have fun together, cry together, and most importantly, grow up together. Liger is a big part of my life that will never be replaced by any other place.

I can feel love, happiness, positivities and growth here. The place that determine who I am.
